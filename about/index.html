    I am a Designer and self-taught Software Engineer focused on data visualization. I believe in the power of well-crafted and carefully designed data products to make information accessible for all. 
          
I currently work as a Data Visualization Engineer at Celonis, creating and maintaining data visualization tools for Process Mining.

My interests include the handmade web, the internet as a house to inhabit, and software development as an act of maintenance and repair. 

You can usually find me at the cinema or playing volleyball in the park.
