I am a Designer and self-taught Software Engineer focused on data visualization. I believe in the power of well-crafted and carefully designed data products to make information accessible for all.

I currently work as a Data Visualization Engineer at [Celonis](https://www.celonis.com/es/), creating and maintaining data visualization tools for Process Mining.

My interests include [the handmade web](https://luckysoap.com/statements/handmadeweb.html), the internet as a [house to inhabit](https://everest-pipkin.com/writing/beautiful_house.pdf), and software development as an act of [maintenance and repair](https://arena-attachments.s3.amazonaws.com/14282920/cac82de5a6c58b067b49d14d0b036ac8.pdf?1638930502).

You can usually find me at the cinema or playing volleyball in the park.
