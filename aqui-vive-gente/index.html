    Aquí vive gente is a collective and community museum project that took its name from the exhibition held by the Brigada Puerta de Tierra in New York.  
        
Aquí vive gente is a phrase taken from posters, billboards and murals that appeared in the Puerta de Tierra neighborhood in San Juan, Puerto Rico, as a resistance to gentrification.
